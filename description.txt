Supported languages: English, 简体中文

[h3]Summary[/h3]
This is a collection adding many kinds of ammunitions for submarine turrets.
Some are merged from other mods that are no longer maintained or hidden by their author. I updated them to make them compatible with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2683843415]BVTA[/url].
The rest are made by myself and they are clearly defined for multiple different purposes.

[h3]Note[/h3]
It is recommended to use this mod with some difficulty mods like: 高难狩猎、T.S.M Missions、EuropaWar
