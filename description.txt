[h3]简介[/h3]
这个合集增加了多种炮台弹药。
有一部分取自其他作者已经停止更新或者隐藏的mod，我重制了它们使其继承我的[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2683843415]原版弹药加强[/url]中的修改。
其他的是由我制作的，有明确用途的弹药。
这个mod也可以单独使用。

[h3]附录[/h3]
推荐与高难mod一起使用，例如：高难狩猎、TSM Mission、EuropaWar

[h3]Summary[/h3]
This is a collection adding many kinds of ammunitions for submarine turrets.
Some are merged from other mods that are no longer maintained or hidden by their author. I remade them to make them inherit changes from [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2683843415]BVTA[/url].
The rest are made by myself and they are clearly defined for multiple different purposes.
This mod can be used without BVTA.

[h3]Note[/h3]
It is recommended to use this mod along with some challenging mods like: T.S.M Missions、EuropaWar
